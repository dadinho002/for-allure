Dadi in Allureland

By:Dadi for Allure

Chapter One: Dadi's magical adventure

In the whimsical realm called Earth, there lived a curious and imaginative boy named Dadi.

Every day, Dadi would venture into the Howling Abyss, a snowy bridge where poros reside, his mind filled with fantasies of finding a girl that would match his banter. He often tried using his only good line to find such a girl, however he had no success.

Dadi was unlike any other child in the realm he had a peculiar fascination with playing League. His favorite champ was none other than The Starry-eyed Songstress- Seraphine.

One sunny afternoon, while venturing Howling Abyss, Dadi stumbled upon a cute and fluffy poro. Before he could pet the Poro,the Poro ran away into a oversized tree stump that seemed out of place. He approached the situation cautiously, and to his amazement, he noticed a small, intricately carved door embedded within the stump.

It was reminiscent of the entrance to Wonderland itself. Excitement surged through Dadi's body. Without hesitation, he pushed the tiny door, and to his astonishment, it swung open, revealing a tunnel that seemed to descend deep into the Earth.

Fueled by curiosity and bravery, Dadi decided to embark on his own magical journey. He took a deep breath and ventured into the mysterious passage. The tunnel twisted and turned, and soon he found himself falling down, down, down.

When he landed, he found himself in a breathtaking garden filled with oversized flowers in vibrant hues. Before him stood a mischievous Poro grinning from ear to ear. The Poro welcomed Dadi to Allureland, a place where imagination and reality intertwined.
